SIMPLE CHOCOLATE CAKE

This recipe is a great recipe to make a simple chocolate cake. It uses ingredients that can be found in most kitchen cupboards. You just need to gather all the ingredients, put them into a big bowl and mix them with a blender or mixer! It can also be used to make cupcakes too, if you adjust the cooking times as mentioned below. Then you can decorate your cake however you'd like to. My favorite is Nutella and sliced banana.

EQUIPMENT

  • Cake tin or muffin tray
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Electric mixer
  • Cooling rack
  • Fork
  • Weighting scales
  • Smaller bowls, to hold ingredients
